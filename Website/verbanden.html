Relations

A few interesting relations

When looking at multivariate plots there was one surprising correlation; teaching and research. It shows that the higher the research score the higher the teaching and vica versa.

The overall score for almost all universities changes each year, but do these changes affect the average overall score per year?

There is little difference in the top 100, but as we look at the lower ranks we see that the average score has inscreased throughout the years. This suggests that universities are improving

On average English-speaking countries score higher than non-English speaking countries. We suspect this may be due to the fact that most English-speaking countries are wealthy and able to put more money into education.
